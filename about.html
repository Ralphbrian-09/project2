About Lutong Pinoy

Our Story

As we observed, we noticed how Filipino recipes were either scattered across old cookbooks, handwritten notes, or shared only through word of mouth. Many modern cooking sites focused on international dishes, while our beloved adobo, sinigang, and kare-kare were often simplified or lost in translation.

So, we built Lutong Pinoy—a place where authentic Filipino flavors take center stage. Whether you're a balikbayan craving home-cooked meals, a curious foodie exploring Filipino cuisine, or a new cook trying to master nanay’s recipes, this site keeps our culinary traditions alive, one dish at a time. Because good food shouldn’t be forgotten—it should be shared, savored, and celebrated. Kain na!✨

Filipino cuisine is a vibrant fusion of flavors influenced by Malay, Chinese, Spanish, and American cultures. Through this platform, we hope to introduce more people to the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines.
